Navigating Undergraduate Expenses:

An Interactive Guide to US Education Costs

By Grace Lam


Educational costs have been rising from year to year, and this has posed concerns to many students planning to study in the United States. Students who want to pursue undergraduate studies in the US might consider the expected expenses of their education as a key factor in selecting universities. Therefore, in this project, I will provide a quick guide to help students understand educational costs from state to state, as well as for different educational lengths and types.

Lets first take a look at the undergraduate expenses in the US from year 2013 to 2021:

As we can see, there is a general increase in the average expenses for undergraduate education in the United States from 2013 to 2021. In fact, there is an over 35 percent increase from 2013 to 2021. With increasing undergraduate expenses, it might be good to start wondering if there is a significant difference across states or a difference between educational lengths.
